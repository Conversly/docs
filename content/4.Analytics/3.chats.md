Access the complete history of every interaction your agent has ever had. Review transcripts, debug issues, and analyze user behavior.

![Chat Logs Dashboard](https://fpfwsswoz0yfjtoz.public.blob.vercel-storage.com/Screenshot%202026-02-20%20at%2012.23.01%E2%80%AFAM.png)

## Conversation List

The sidebar provides a real-time feed of all conversations.

- **Search**: Quickly find specific chats by message content or user ID.
- **Channel Filters**:
    - **All**: View every conversation across all platforms.
    - **Widget**: Chats from your website.
    - **WA**: Chats from WhatsApp.
    - **Other**: Chats from other sources (API, Voice, etc.).

## Conversation Detail

Click on any chat to see the full transcript and metadata.

![Conversation Detail](https://fpfwsswoz0yfjtoz.public.blob.vercel-storage.com/Screenshot%202026-02-20%20at%2012.27.30%E2%80%AFAM.png)

- **Message History**: View the exact exchange between the user and the AI.
- **Timestamps**: See when each message was sent and received.
- **Source**: Identify where the message originated (e.g., `WIDGET`).

## Export & Analyze

- **Filter**: Apply advanced filters (Date Range, Sentiment, Status) to narrow down your search.
- **Download JSON**: Export the full conversation data for offline analysis or compliance.
