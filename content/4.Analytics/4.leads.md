VerlyAI isn't just for support; it's a growth engine. The **Leads** page allows you to track, filter, and export potential customers captured by your AI agent.

## Leads Dashboard

The dashboard provides a centralized view of all leads generated from conversations.

![Leads Dashboard](https://fpfwsswoz0yfjtoz.public.blob.vercel-storage.com/Screenshot%202026-02-19%20at%2011.23.09%E2%80%AFPM.png)

### Searching & Filtering

Easily find the leads you're looking for with powerful search and filter tools:

- **Search**: Enter a user's name or email address (e.g., `shivam0xverma@gmail.com`) to find specific records.
- **Source**: Filter leads by their origin channel (e.g., Web, WhatsApp).
- **Topic**: Narrow down leads based on the conversation topic (e.g., "Pricing Inquiry", "Product Demo").
- **Date Range**: View leads generated within a specific timeframe (From/To).

### Lead Details

The leads table displays key information for each captured prospect:

- **Contact Info**: The primary contact details, such as email address or phone number.
- **Communication**: A summary or link to the conversation where the lead was captured.
- **Source**: The platform where the interaction took place.
- **Created At**: The exact date and time the lead was generated.

## Export & Integration

You can export your leads for further analysis or sync them with your CRM:

- **Export to CSV**: Download the entire list for offline use.
- **CRM Integration**: Automatically push new leads to Salesforce, HubSpot, or Pipedrive.
