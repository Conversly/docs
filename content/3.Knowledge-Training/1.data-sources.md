VerlyAI uses RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) to ground your agent's responses in your specific business data. This ensures accurate, on-brand answers without hallucinations.

## Adding Knowledge Sources

Your agent needs specific knowledge about your business to answer questions accurately. Navigate to the **Data Sources** tab in your agent's dashboard.

![Knowledge Base Tab](https://fpfwsswoz0yfjtoz.public.blob.vercel-storage.com/Screenshot%202026-02-19%20at%2010.05.56%E2%80%AFPM.png)

Click on the **Add Knowledge** button. You can add knowledge sources using four different methods:

![Add Knowledge Options](https://fpfwsswoz0yfjtoz.public.blob.vercel-storage.com/Screenshot%202026-02-19%20at%2010.06.18%E2%80%AFPM.png)

### Uploading Documents

Select the **Documents** option to upload your files.

![Upload Documents](https://fpfwsswoz0yfjtoz.public.blob.vercel-storage.com/Screenshot%202026-02-19%20at%2010.07.07%E2%80%AFPM.png)

## Capacity & Usage

Data usage is measured in **Pages** (normalized units of text).

| Plan | Capacity | Sync Frequency |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Starter** | 1,000 Pages | Manual |
| **Growth** | 10,000 Pages | Monthly Auto-Refresh |
| **Enterprise** | 500,000 Pages | Daily Auto-Refresh |

## Management

### Re-training
When you update a data source (e.g., change your pricing page), you must **re-train** the agent.
- **Manual**: Click "Train Agent" in the dashboard.
- **Auto**: Enabled for Growth and Enterprise plans.

### Exclusions
You can exclude specific URLs or file sections to prevent the agent from learning sensitive or outdated information.
