## The Architecture Difference

The fundamental distinction between these platforms isn't feature lists—it's architecture:

**Intercom** was built in 2011 as a live chat platform. AI (Fin) was added in 2023 as a feature layer. The platform still revolves around human agents managing conversations, with AI as an assistant. This legacy shows in the per-seat pricing, complex setup, and incremental AI capabilities.

**VerlyAI** was built in 2024 as an AI agent platform from day one. The entire system is designed around autonomous agents resolving queries, with human escalation as a safety net. This shows in the native RAG architecture, unlimited concurrency, usage-based pricing, and the documented 80% resolution rate.
